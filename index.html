WHERE COMFORT meets quality

Functional layout

We use every square meter to the maximum, while nothing interferes with comfortable movement, for example, protruding furniture or wires underfoot. We avoid narrow and long corridors and cramped rooms where space is used inefficiently, and we isolate the hallway so that dirt from shoes does not spread throughout the apartment.
